GODSBANEN by VARIOUS ARTISTS

Karen Wegeners Gade 8, 8000 Aarhus
Share this spot

Description

Godsbanen is a hidden gem of Aarhus’ street art scene, where walls come alive with ever-changing murals and graffiti. Every visit is a chance to discover something new and unexpected.

Once an old industrial complex, it now hosts workshops, exhibitions and performance spaces — while the exterior walls act as a living open-air gallery. From bold murals to small tags, it’s a place to wander, explore and soak in the city’s underground creative energy.
