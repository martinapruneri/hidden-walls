Street Art Adventures in Aarhus

ABOUT US

Aarhus is full of stories painted on its walls and I'm here to help you uncover them.

This platform brings together hidden murals, local stories and community tips all in one place.

Whether you're a curious traveler, a student or just someone who loves discovering new corners of the city, I want to help you dive into Aarhus' urban art scene with confidence, curiosity and a sense of adventure.

Join the community, share your finds and explore the city in a whole new way.

WHAT'S YOUR EXPLORATION
STYLE

No time to lose

Short on time? Use the map in THE WALLS to find spots quickly.

Each spot has an address and a Google Maps link, so you can get there without missing a beat and still enjoy the city’s hidden murals.

Go with the flow

If you love discovering things by chance, this is for you!

Browse the artworks in THE WALLS, let the visuals inspire you and explore at your own pace. No strict directions, just curiosity, good vibes and hidden art around every corner.
