SYDHAVNEN by VARIOUS ARTISTS

Kalkværksvej 17, 8000 Aarhus
Share this spot

Description

Sydhavnen is a hidden gem for street art lovers, where vibrant murals and creative tags transform industrial walls into an urban canvas.

Sydhavnen offers a raw and authentic street art experience in Aarhus. This area, once dominated by warehouses and docks, now showcases a mix of colourful murals, bold graffiti and experimental pieces that constantly evolve. Walking through Sydhavnen, you'll discover both large-scale works and smaller, intricate tags tucked into corners. The neighborhood's gritty industrial vibe adds to the underground energy, making it a favorite spot for photographers, locals and urban explorers alike. Nearby streets also host additional pieces, encouraging visitors to wander and uncover more hidden gems along the way.

Artists

Peter Birk

Peter Birk is a Danish street artist known for his vibrant, expressive murals that blend bold colors with dynamic forms. His work often explores urban life, personal stories and social commentary, leaving a lasting mark on the walls of Aarhus and beyond.

David Hernan

David Hernan is a contemporary street artist recognized for his intricate, visually striking murals. Combining geometric patterns with urban storytelling, his work brings life to public spaces and transforms everyday walls into immersive art experiences.
